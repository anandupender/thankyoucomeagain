Thank You Come Again

TYCA is like an In-House Design Team for your next big idea.

We’ve worked behind the scenes as Operators, Event Planners, and Line Cooks to bring a sense of Service to our work as Designers.

“Thank You Come Again”, or TYCA, is a playful reclaiming of the racialized catchphrase of Simpsons character, Apu. Using this name creates space to question and reimagine South Asian stereotypes and is an intentional celebration of South Asian creativity in service of society.
